Here are some links that I find interesting and useful for doing research, etc.

Publication-quality figure tools

  • gnuplot: gnuplot is highly recommended to draw publication-quality figures. One of its advantages is the performance, it can draw multiple points fast.

  • matplotlib: A MATLAB-like drawing tool for making publication-quality figures. (by John Hunter, 1968-2012)

  • TikZ: Let's “draw” best figures by programming LaTeX.

  • matlab2tikz: A converter that takes a MATLAB(R) figure and spits out a TikZ (Pgfplots) figure for smooth integration into LaTeX. (by Nico Schlomer)

Website design tools

  • jemdoc: This website is made by jemdoc. (by Jacob Mattingley)

  • jemdoc+mathjax: An extension of jemdoc mainly support mathjax. (by Wonseok Shin)

  • PubList: JabRef export to List of Publications in HTML (jemdoc) (by Truong Nghiem)
